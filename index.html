About

My name is Daquan Martin, and I live in Tampa, FL. I am a front-end Web Developer and Software Developer. I have a Bachelor of Science in IT Management from Keiser University. Web Development is what I'm most interested in and what drives me to study. In my free time, I study how people and computers work together and how people and computers affect each other. I am driven and self-motivated, and I am always trying out new technologies and ways of doing things. I am very interested in Web Development and work hard to improve myself and the development community as a whole.

Skills


Almost every day, I spend the whole day experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also play around with Bootstrap and ReactJs, and I try to make websites that are both fun and useful. I'm interested in learning new things, and I like work that pushes me in a different direction. I try to keep up with changes in the state of the art so I can meet challenges with tools that work well for the task at hand. If you look at the projects I follow on GitHub, you can get a good idea of what kinds of tools I like to use.
